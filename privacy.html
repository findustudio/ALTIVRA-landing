Privacy Policy

Effective April 19, 2026

01Who we are

Altivra is a pilot productivity tool built by a working airline pilot. We collect the minimum data required to run the product, treat your data the way we'd want our own handled, and never sell it. This policy explains what we collect, why, where it lives, and what rights you have over it.

"Altivra", "we", "us", and "our" refer to Altivra (operating under Findustudio). "You" refers to any pilot using the website (altivra.app) or the iPad app.

02Information we collect

Waitlist (website)

  • Email address — when you submit the beta access form
  • Technical metadata — timestamp, browser user agent, referrer (for spam prevention and to understand where visitors come from)

Account (iPad app)

  • Name, email, password hash — when you create an account
  • Flight logbook data — entries you add, import, or enrich
  • Roster data — imported from your airline's crew management system
  • Fatigue data — rest periods, sleep markers, layover context
  • Pay data — payslip information you upload for verification against your contract
  • Preferences & settings — your configuration of the app

Device & diagnostic data

  • iPad model, iOS version, app version (for compatibility and crash reports)
  • Anonymized usage analytics — which features you open, session frequency. This data is never tied to your personal identity.

03How we use your data

  • Deliver the app's features (roster display, fatigue tracking, pay verification, etc.)
  • Send beta access emails to waitlist subscribers
  • Troubleshoot issues using anonymized crash reports
  • Improve the app through aggregate usage analytics
  • Respond to your support requests

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or training third-party AI models on your personal information.

04Where your data lives

  • Flight, logbook, pay, and fatigue data — stored in your iCloud, encrypted end-to-end. We, as a company, do not have access to the contents.
  • Waitlist emails, account identifiers, Firebase Auth — stored in Google Firebase (Google Cloud), encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Cached data on your iPad — encrypted by iOS using standard Apple data protection

05Third parties we work with

Altivra is small. To run the product, we rely on carefully selected third parties. We send them only the data they need to do their job.

  • Google / Firebase — authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Functions. Processes account and waitlist data on our behalf under Google's DPA.
  • FlightAware (Captain tier only) — receives your flight number and date to enrich your logbook with real routing, block times, and gate info. No personal identifiers are sent.
  • Anthropic (Captain tier, AI Copilot, coming 2026) — processes your natural-language queries. We send only the minimum context needed to answer each question; queries are not used to train Anthropic's models (per Anthropic's enterprise policy).
  • Apple — handles iCloud encrypted sync and App Store subscription payments. Governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • Vercel — hosts the altivra.app website (where you're reading this). Processes server logs with standard access information.

06What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data. Ever.
  • We don't share your data with your airline, your carrier, or any other aviation employer.
  • We don't show ads. Not now, not at scale.
  • We don't track you across the web — no cross-site cookies, no fingerprinting.
  • We don't use your content to train AI on a population level.

07Your rights

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you. Email legal@altivra.io.
  • Delete — delete your account and all associated data. In-app, one tap: Settings → Account → Delete account. For website-waitlist deletion, email contact@altivra.io.
  • Export — download your logbook (CSV, PDF), roster, pay history, and fatigue log at any time from within the app.
  • Correct — edit any data directly through the app.
  • Object / restrict — if you're in a jurisdiction that grants these rights (EU/UK GDPR, California CCPA, etc.), contact us and we'll honor your request within 30 days.

08Cookies

The website uses only functional storage (no tracking cookies, no analytics cookies). The iPad app uses no cookies — it uses Apple's native secure storage.

09Children's privacy

Altivra is intended for commercial pilots, who are adults by professional requirement. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we learn we've accidentally collected such data, we'll delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who believe we've collected data about their child can email legal@altivra.io.

10Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll notify you by email (for account holders) or in-app announcement at least 30 days before the changes take effect. The most recent version is always live at altivra.app/privacy.

11Contact

Questions about this policy, or requests to access, correct, or delete your data:

  • Privacy & data requests: legal@altivra.io
  • General support: support@altivra.io
  • Contact: contact@altivra.io
One honest note. This policy is written in plain language on purpose — legal documents that nobody reads protect nobody. If anything here is unclear, ambiguous, or feels like it's hiding something, please email legal@altivra.io and we'll clarify.
